Cookie Notice

Cookies and similar technologies on this corporate website

Last updated: May 3, 2026

This notice explains how Yixi Technology Trading (Shanghai) Co., Ltd. (“we”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our public website (the “Site”). The Site presents our capabilities in precision instruments, technology promotion, training, local support, and international trade, including references to brands such as ZYGO, TSI, and AMETEK. For how we process personal data more broadly, see our Privacy Notice.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a site. They can remember preferences, session state, or support analytics. Similar technologies include local storage and pixels.

2. Our current approach

The Site is primarily informational and lead-generation focused. We do not operate cross-site behavioural advertising cookies or ad-network tracking as of the date above. If we introduce analytics, chat widgets, or other features that rely on non-essential cookies, we will update this notice and, where required, provide appropriate consent or choice mechanisms.

3. Types you might encounter

  • Strictly necessary — needed for basic display, security, or simple form operation.
  • Functional — for example remembering a language preference where offered.
  • Analytics — if added later, typically in aggregate form to understand traffic and errors so we can improve content.

4. Third parties

Resources such as hosted web fonts (e.g. Google Fonts) or form relay services may process technical data under their own policies. Contact forms routed through a relay provider may set session cookies governed by that provider. You can review your browser or extension settings to limit third-party requests.

5. Managing cookies

Browsers let you delete or block cookies and sometimes warn before storing them. Restricting cookies may affect form submission or other interactive features. Refer to your browser vendor’s documentation for steps.

6. Updates

We may revise this notice as the Site evolves; the “Last updated” date will change accordingly.

7. Contact

Questions: Contact us or email info@yixi-tech.com.
