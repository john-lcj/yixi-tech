About Yixi Technology

Rooted in Shanghai FTZ, bridging global technology with China's market

Company Profile

Global Brands.
Local Partnership, End to End.

Yixi Technology Trading (Shanghai) Co., Ltd. is registered in the China (Shanghai) Pilot Free Trade Zone. We are more than a supplier: we are a technology and service partner in precision optical metrology, cleanroom & particle applications, and electrochemical analysis—helping R&D, quality, and production teams turn accurate, traceable measurements into confidence on the floor.

Working with leading brands including ZYGO, TSI, and AMETEK, we support semiconductor, aerospace, research labs, pharma & controlled environments, new energy, and advanced equipment—guiding you from selection and proof-of-concept through acceptance, so world-class hardware fits how China innovates and scales.

This mirrors how we operate day to day: market insight & positioning, technical promotion & training, localized lifecycle support, and international trade—one accountable team from first conversation through delivery — acceptance — after-sales.
